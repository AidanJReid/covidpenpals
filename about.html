About Us

We created Covid Penpals as a way for the vulnerable, especially those living in nursing homes, to make a connection with others facing similar difficulties.

Our Mission

Connecting the vulnerable through the medium of writing. Empowering the needy and vulnerable through this difficult time and make real human connections with others who are facing hardship.

History

  • March 2020. Covid 19 declared a pandemic
  • April 2020. Irish Government recognise the impact for the first time on nursing/care homes
  • December 2020. Creation of Covid PenPals.
