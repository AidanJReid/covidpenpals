The Covid-19 pandemic has highlighted the paramount importance of public health. But it is not the only crisis we currently face. Before and during the Covid era, many people, particularly the most vulnerable members of our society have been affected by a parallel, silent public-health crisis - lonlieness. According to research from the US, lonlieness can increase the risk of premature mortality and can be twice as harmful to physical and mental health as obesity.

Here at Covid Penpals we are bringing people together to combat lonlieness. Our organisation is entirely volunteer-operated but we nonetheless incur expenses for the professional maintenance of our website. These expenses help to make Covid Penpals into the comprehensive, user-friendly experience that our members need and deserve. Please consider donating to help us maintain the service we have built. When you make a donation to Covid Penpals, you are joining a community of people who are providing for, protecting and connecting some of the most vulnerable people in our society.
